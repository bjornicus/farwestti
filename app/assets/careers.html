We currently have openings for the following positions:

Project Manager

This position you would work as a liaison between project owner, architect, superintendent, subcontractors and suppliers to ensure a smooth and safe completion of the project that exceeds the client’s expectations.

Required Knowledge, Skills and Experience

  • 5+ years experience as estimator and/or project manager in commercial construction
  • B.A. in Construction Management is strongly preferred
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills and ability to manage several projects concurrently

Working Superintendent

This position you are responsible for planning, coordinating and overseeing all on-site activities in accordance with the requirements of the project.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manages site activities, manpower, equipment, subcontractors and material to be efficient and cost effective
  • Communicates with the owner, design team and Wilcox project manager to effectively convey necessary information to and from the field
  • Works closely with project manager and project engineer to track subcontractor progress, budget, schedule, RFI’s, shop drawings, submittals, plan revisions and change orders
  • Communicates with field operations manager to fulfill manpower, safety and equipment requirements as needed
  • Supervises all Farwest employees and subcontractors working on the assigned project
  • Creates and enforces Site Specific Safety Plan
  • Responsible for scheduling inspections and testing, quality control, conforming to local building code and ADA requirements

Required Knowledge, Skills and Experience

  • 2+ years construction experience in commercial construction. Restaurant and retail construction experience are preferred.
  • Knowledge of all tools, materials and methods involved in commercial site-work and building construction
  • Understands and is able to apply construction scheduling principles
  • Ability to read and interpret drawings and specifications, and shop drawings
  • Basic computer skills and a moderate level of experience with MS Office and MS Project
  • Creates and maintains project schedule and daily logs
  • Experienced at Metal Stud Framing and Drywall
  • Skilled at hanging doors and Relites
  • Working in occupied buildings (must be an excellent communicator)
